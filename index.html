About MoodRadar

MoodRadar is a health app designed to help you understand and manage your personal stress levels. Take our quick test three times a day – in the morning, at midday, and in the evening – to track your stress trends for the current day, the past 7 days, and the last 30 days.

Our test comprises 7 questions that adjust based on the time of day, followed by a simple selfie. Using advanced Face API technology, we calculate your stress level based on your responses and facial cues. Please note that the resulting stress level is an approximation, not a 100% guarantee.

Additionally, MoodRadar offers a variety of guided exercises to help you reduce stress and relax. We continuously work to improve our service so that you can achieve a balanced, stress-free life.
