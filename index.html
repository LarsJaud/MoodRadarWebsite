About MoodRadar

MoodRadar is a health app designed to help you understand and manage your personal stress levels. Take our quick test three times a day – in the morning, at midday, and in the evening – to track your stress trends for the current day, the past 7 days, and the last 30 days.

Our test comprises 7 questions that adjust based on the time of day, followed by a simple selfie. Using advanced Face API technology, we calculate your stress level based on your responses and facial cues. Please note that the resulting stress level is an approximation, not a 100% guarantee.

Additionally, MoodRadar offers a variety of guided exercises to help you reduce stress and relax. We continuously work to improve our service so that you can achieve a balanced, stress-free life.

3. Processing of Personal Data

3.1 Registration and User Account

During registration via Apple ID, the following data is collected:

  • Apple ID
  • Email address

3.2 User Data

  • Account Information: First name, last name, email, bio (optional)
  • Stress Data: Stress level, time of day of measurement, date and time of measurement

4. Data Storage and Security

Personal data is stored only as long as necessary for the outlined purposes or as required by law. Data is encrypted and stored securely.

Facial data is not stored and is only used during real-time analysis via the Google Cloud Vision API. Once analyzed, the image is discarded. No biometric data is stored or shared.

5. Use of Third-Party Services

5.1 Third-Party Sharing

We use Google Cloud services for storage and facial recognition (Face API). Data may be transferred outside the EU, where Google ensures compliance through adequate safeguards. More details in Google's Privacy Policy.

5.2 Face API Usage

The **Google Cloud Vision API** is used to analyze facial expressions. A user-provided image is processed in real-time to recognize emotions such as happiness, sadness, or anger. This is used only to compute a stress level. After analysis, the image is immediately discarded. No biometric data is stored or shared.
